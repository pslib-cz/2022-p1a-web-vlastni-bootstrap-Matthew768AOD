Learn more about select species of Sea Cucumbers!

Holothuria edulis

Also known as the edible sea cucumber, is a popular species that is harvested for food in many countries. It is found in the Indo-Pacific region and can grow up to 40 cm in length.

Synapta maculata

Synapta maculata is a long, thin species of sea cucumber that can reach up to 50 cm in length. It is found in shallow waters in the Atlantic Ocean and is known for its ability to burrow into sand or mud.

Thelenota ananas

Commonly known as the pineapple sea cucumber due to its yellow, bumpy appearance, is found in the Indo-Pacific region. It can grow up to 30 cm in length and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Stichopus horrens

Also known as the black sea cucumber, is a species found in the Indo-Pacific region. It can grow up to 50 cm in length and is known for its black or dark brown color.

Cucumaria frondosa

Cucumaria frondosa is a species of sea cucumber found in the North Atlantic Ocean. It has a reddish-brown color and can grow up to 30 cm in length. It is sometimes called the "sea strawberry" because of its appearance.

Bohadschia argus

Bohadschia argus is a species of sea cucumber found in the Indo-Pacific region. It can grow up to 50 cm in length and is known for its striking blue color with black spots. It is sometimes called the "blue sea cucumber" or the "argus sea cucumber".
